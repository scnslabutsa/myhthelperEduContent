<!---title: How is tamoxifen taken<sup>3</sup>? --->
---

* Tamoxifen comes in pill form.
* You take one pill every day, if possible at the same time.
* It is taken for 5-10 years.

***Common tamoxifen brand names***
* Nolvadex
* Tomafen
